About the Project

The Melissodes Project is a online resource dedicated to information about the genus of bee Melissodes. This project focusus on research, taxonomy, phylogeny, morphological traits, and natural history of these bees. Here you'll be able to find scientific articles about each species, and the genus as a whole. You can also find detailed figures and diagrams of all things Melissodes.
